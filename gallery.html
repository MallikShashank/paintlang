The Coded Gallery masterpieces as living programs Open the studio

Every painting below is public-domain art traced into Paintlang code: masses, light, and thousands of brush strokes, organised into named layers. Open one and it loads as a program in your browser - read it, replay it painting itself, hide the sky, change one word to repaint it in watercolour, or copy a layer into your own work. Images courtesy of the Art Institute of Chicago's open access program (CC0).

Built with Paintlang, the live-coding painting studio where the code and the canvas are the same artwork. Trace your own images the same way - free, in your browser.
