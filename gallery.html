The Coded Gallery

A museum where every painting is a living program. Walk the rooms in the order history painted them, from Edo in 1831 to Paris at the turn of the century. Rest your eyes on any canvas and it will paint itself for you, stroke by stroke. Open any of them as code, and it is yours to remix.
